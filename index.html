Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

PantryAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store your personal photos on our servers. Images are processed by Google's Gemini AI to identify ingredients and are immediately discarded. Your dietary preferences and saved recipes are stored locally on your device.

2. AI Processing

PantryAI uses Google Gemini API to analyze images and generate content. This processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share your name or identity with the AI model.

3. Storage

The app uses local storage (AsyncStorage and SecureStore) to save your preferences and API key. No account registration is required, meaning your data stays on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

PantryAI communicates with Google's Gemini API for AI-powered features including ingredient identification, recipe generation, cooking tips, and text-to-speech. No personal identifying information is sent to these services beyond the image data you explicitly choose to scan.

5. Children's Privacy

PantryAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
